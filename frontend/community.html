Just got back from Bali! 🌴 Pro tip: Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces early morning (before 8 AM) to avoid crowds. The sunrise view is absolutely magical. Also, book your Uluwatu Temple tour separately — it's way cheaper than resort packages!
